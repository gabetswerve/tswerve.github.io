BIO

Based in Vancouver, BC, independent singer, rapper, and producer T Swerve is carving out a highly unique lane in alternative hip-hop and melodic R&B. Moving past the raw underground trap of his 2020 debut Psychology 101, T Swerve's maturity centers around his landmark album Welcome 2 My World, a project that highlights his smooth vocal cadences and willingness to push musical boundaries.

His creative independence is fully realized on the Bird's Eye View EP, a project he produced almost entirely by himself. Handling his own instrumentation allowed him to meticulously sculpt a dark, multi-layered sonic landscape that elevates him far beyond a standard vocalist. This trajectory culminates in his two latest 2026 singles, which perfectly balance his dual identities. "Forever!" highlights his spacious, atmospheric R&B lane, while his absolute latest track, "IDFWU," returns to a defiant, hyper-precise rap pocket. From self-producing entire EPs to dropping sharp modern singles, T Swerve remains an exceptional, self-contained musical architect.
